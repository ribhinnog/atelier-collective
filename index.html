A space for artists to create, connect, and grow together.

We’re a small community of local creatives — painters, designers, photographers, illustrators — who meet to share work, inspire one another, and celebrate the process of making.

Who we are

Founded in 2023, The Atelier Collective began as a handful of artists meeting in a shared studio space once a month. Today, we’re a growing group of creatives who value the quiet beauty of craftsmanship and the joy of learning from one another.

We believe in collaboration over competition, and we open our doors to anyone who creates — from students and hobbyists to professionals looking to reconnect with their roots.
